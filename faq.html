Frequently Asked Questions

General

What is PChat?

PChat is a fast, secure messaging app built for local city communities.

Is PChat free?

Yes! PChat is completely free to download and use.

Which platforms is PChat available on?

PChat is currently available for iOS on the App Store.

Account

How do I create an account?

Download PChat from the App Store, open the app, and follow the registration steps.

Can I change my username?

Yes! Go to Settings > Edit Profile to change your username at any time.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings > Privacy and Security > Delete My Account.

Secret Chats

What are Secret Chats?

Secret Chats provide end-to-end encryption, meaning only you and the recipient can read the messages.

How do I start a Secret Chat?

Open a contact's profile page and tap "Start Secret Chat".

Can Secret Chat messages be forwarded?

No. Messages in Secret Chats cannot be forwarded.

Groups & Communities

What's the maximum group size?

PChat groups can hold up to 200,000 members.

How do I find local groups?

Use the "People Nearby" feature to discover groups and users in your area.

Can I create a private group?

Yes! You can create both public and private groups.

Privacy & Security

Is my data sold to advertisers?

Absolutely not. PChat does not sell, share, or monetize your personal data.

How can I block someone?

Open the user's profile page and tap "Block User".

How do I report inappropriate content?

Long-press on a message or open a user/group profile and tap "Report".
