Frequently Asked Questions

Last updated: March 25, 2026

General Questions

What is PChat?

PChat is a fast and secure messaging app built for local city communities. It lets you send messages, voice notes, photos, videos, and files while connecting you with people and groups in your neighborhood.

Is PChat free?

Yes, PChat is completely free to download and use. There are no subscription fees or hidden charges for core messaging features.

Which platforms does PChat support?

PChat is currently available for iOS. We are working on bringing PChat to more platforms in the future.

Account

How do I create an account?

Download PChat from the App Store, open the app, and register with your phone number. You'll receive a verification code via SMS to complete the setup.

Can I change my phone number?

Yes, you can change your phone number in Settings without losing your chats, groups, or contacts.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. Go to Settings → Privacy and Security → Delete My Account. This will permanently remove your account, messages, and all associated data from our servers.

Secret Chats

What are Secret Chats?

Secret Chats use end-to-end encryption, meaning only you and the recipient can read the messages. They are not stored on our servers, cannot be forwarded, and support self-destructing messages with a timer.

How do I start a Secret Chat?

Open a contact's profile, tap "More" (···), and select "Start Secret Chat."

How do I verify the encryption?

Each Secret Chat has a unique encryption key visualized as an image. Compare this image with your chat partner (in person or via a trusted channel) to verify that no man-in-the-middle attack is taking place.

Groups & Communities

How do I create a group?

Tap the compose button (pencil icon) on the chat list, select "New Group," add members, and set a group name.

What are the group limits?

A standard group can have up to 200 members. Supergroups can support larger communities.

How do I find local groups?

Use the search feature to find public groups in your area, or check the "Nearby" section to discover groups and users around you.

Privacy & Security

Who can see my phone number?

By default, your phone number is only visible to people in your contacts. You can change this in Settings → Privacy and Security → Phone Number.

Can I block someone?

Yes. Open the user's profile, tap "More" (···), and select "Block User." Blocked users cannot send you messages or see your online status.

How do I report abuse?

Long-press on an offensive message in a chat, select "Report," and choose a reason. You can also report a user or group from their profile page.
